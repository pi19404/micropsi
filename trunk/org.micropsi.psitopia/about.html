What is Psitopia?



main page

Psitopia is an installation of MicroPsi components that allows users to send agents to a common, simulated world on the web.

Psitopia uses just the standard core components of any MicroPsi system, plus some web-application stuff. Why are we doing this? Because we can, and because we want to show.
Now it's your turn: We are looking for skilled JavaScript programmers and world designers who want to make Psitopia something unique. What kind of work would have to be done?
  • Creative: Design a large, complex, unique-looking, beautiful and dynamic world for our little steamvehicles to roam
  • JavaScript: Implement clever ways of displaying the world in a browser. (You know Google Maps? That's how it should look like, methinks. Even if it's not Google Maps, we need ways of displaying large worlds.)
  • Java/JavaScript: Make Psitopia into a web application that allows people to create agents from their browsers, watch them and see them perform in Psitopia.
